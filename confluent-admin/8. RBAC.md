# Role Based Access Control

## Terminology

- Principal: A user, group, application, or service

- **dc:** "Domain Component"

  ex: dc=confluent,dc=io

  ex: dc=google,dc=com

- **ou:** "Organizational Unit"

  ex: ou=groups

- **cn:** "Common Name"

  ex: cn=devs

- **dn:** "Distinguished Name"

  ex: dn:cn=devs,ou=groups,dc=confluent,dc=io
